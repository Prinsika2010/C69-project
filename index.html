School Church
Was established in 1978

“A community school with a distinctive Christian character.” Long before the government became involved in providing education for everyone in our country, the Church of England had a vision that it wanted every parish to have a school for the education of poor children.



School Library
Was built in 1980

A school library is a library within a school where students, staff, and often, parents of a public or private school have access to a variety of resources.



School Auditorium
Was built in 1982

An auditorium is a room built to enable an audience to hear and watch performances. For movie theatres, the number of auditoria (or auditoriums) is expressed as the number of screens.
